Goa


Goa is a very beautiful and popular tourist destination in India, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  1. Calangute Beach – One of the largest and most popular beaches in Goa.
  2. Fort Aguada – A 17th-century Portuguese fort with a lighthouse offering scenic views.
  3. Baga Beach – Famous for nightlife, water sports, and beach parties.
  4. Dudhsagar Falls – A majestic four-tiered waterfall nestled in the Western Ghats.
  5. Old Goa Churches – Home to beautiful colonial-era churches like Basilica of Bom Jesus.
  • Prawn Curry – A spicy coconut-based seafood curry loved across Goa.
  • Fish Recheado – Fried fish stuffed with a tangy red masala paste.
  • Goan Xacuti – A flavorful curry made with roasted spices and coconut.
  • Vindaloo – A spicy-sour pork dish with vinegar and garlic.
  • Bebinca – A traditional layered Goan dessert made from coconut milk and sugar.
